Commitment to your privacy

'Offline AI Pro' is committed to protecting your privacy. Unlike cloud solutions, all your data stays on your phone. We have no backend servers.

1. Data collection

'Offline AI Pro' does not collect any personally identifiable data. The application works completely offline and processes all data locally on your device.

Locally stored data:

  • Uploaded documents: PDF, Word files, scanned images
  • Conversations: AI-generated questions and answers
  • AI models: Automatically downloaded for local processing
  • User preferences: Application settings

âœ… Guarantee: None of this data is ever transmitted to our servers or third parties. Everything remains exclusively on your device.

2. Data usage

Your data is only used to provide application features:

  • Analyze your documents with AI to answer your questions
  • Save conversations to maintain context
  • Improve user experience (local preferences)

3. Data sharing

ðŸš« 'Offline AI Pro' NEVER shares your data with anyone.

Since the application works completely offline and without a backend server, there is technically no possibility of sharing your data.

4. In-app purchases (IAP)

For the Premium version, purchases are managed by:

  • Google Play: Google handles payments and subscriptions

These platforms may collect data according to their own privacy policies. We only receive purchase confirmation to activate Premium features.

5. Data security

Your data is protected by:

  • Local encryption: All data is encrypted on your device
  • No cloud: No network transmission of sensitive data
  • Total control: You keep total control of your data
  • Open source: Code is publicly auditable

6. GDPR compliance

'Offline AI Pro' is fully GDPR compliant:

  • No collection: No personal data is collected
  • No processing: No data processing on our servers
  • Right to erasure: Simply delete the application
  • Transparency: Open source and auditable code

7. Data deletion

To permanently delete all your data:

  1. Open your phone settings
  2. Go to "Applications" or "Apps"
  3. Search for and select "Offline AI Pro"
  4. Select "Uninstall" or "Delete"

This action immediately and permanently deletes all your data, documents and conversations stored locally.

8. Updates

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in our practices. Significant modifications will be notified to you via the application or our website.
