Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Offline AI Pro.

Does the app work offline?

Yes, completely! 'Offline AI Pro' works 100% offline. AI models are downloaded once and then executed locally on your phone. No internet connection is required to ask questions about your documents.

Is my data really secure?

Absolutely. Unlike cloud solutions, 'Offline AI Pro' processes everything locally. Your documents, questions and answers never leave your phone. We have no servers, no cloud databases, and we don't collect any personal data.

What document formats are supported?

'Offline AI Pro' supports PDF (native and scanned), Word documents (.docx), and images (JPG, PNG). For scanned PDFs and images, advanced OCR automatically extracts text with over 95% accuracy.

Is the app fast on my phone?

Yes! Models are optimized for mobile with 4-bit quantization and native ARM64 execution. On a recent phone, answers arrive in 2-5 seconds. The app also preserves battery life thanks to specific optimizations.

Can I choose different AI models?

Absolutely! The app offers multiple AI models to choose from based on your needs: Gemma 3 1B (fast and lightweight), Llama 3.2 1B (versatile), Phi-3.5 Mini (powerful), Qwen 0.5B (ultra-light), and Mistral 7B (premium performance).

Can I customize AI generation settings?

Yes! Advanced users can fine-tune generation parameters including temperature, top-k, top-p, max tokens, and context window. The app also monitors RAM usage and prevents crashes by checking available memory before loading models.

Why uncensored AI models?

Because your AI shouldn't be your babysitter. Commercial AI models are formatted for consensus and blocked legitimate research questions. Our uncensored models give you intellectual freedom - you're the judge of what you want to learn. 100% private, 100% free.
