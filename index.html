Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AskTheDocs.

Does the app work offline?

Yes, completely! AskTheDocs works 100% offline. AI models are downloaded once and then executed locally on your phone. No internet connection is required to ask questions about your documents.

Is my data really secure?

Absolutely. Unlike cloud solutions, AskTheDocs processes everything locally. Your documents, questions and answers never leave your phone. We have no servers, no cloud databases, and we don't collect any personal data.

What document formats are supported?

AskTheDocs supports PDF (native and scanned), Word documents (.docx), and images (JPG, PNG). For scanned PDFs and images, advanced OCR automatically extracts text with over 95% accuracy.

Is the app fast on my phone?

Yes! Models are optimized for mobile with 4-bit quantization and native ARM64 execution. On a recent phone, answers arrive in 2-5 seconds. The app also preserves battery life thanks to specific optimizations.
